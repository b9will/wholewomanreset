Frequently Asked Questions

Clarity before you begin.

Who is this work for?

This programme is designed for women ready to create meaningful change in their life, to feel better: mind, body, spirit. There’s a wide range of goals that women who work with us have. Women wanting support with their energy levels, hormonal health, anxiety/panic/low mood, gut issues or any combination of these.  Regardless of your primary concern, it is for women who are wanting deeper, foundational, root cause, bespoke support.

What Conditions do you Treat?

First and foremost, we treat the person sitting in front of us. That being said, if you come in with particular diagnoses, here are some that we often work with: PCOS, endometriosis, PMS, PMDD, hypothalamic amenorrhea, IBS, SIBO, mold illness, IBD, anxiety, depression, and PTSD. We can ensure we're the right fit for your concerns via the application.

Are any supplements or testing included in the programme?

If supplements or testing are recommended, it would be in addition to the programme. But Heather will discuss what needs to be prioritised based on your financial needs.

How long is the programme / will we be working together?

Our work typically spans 3 to 6 months. This allows us to work at a pace that supports your mindset shifts, body-based practices and any lab work with Heather, ensuring you get personalised guidance and lasting results.

What is the difference between coaching and therapy?

Coaching is future-focused and action-oriented. We work together to explore what’s holding you back, shift limiting beliefs and create practical strategies to move forward. Therapy focuses on processing past experiences and mental health treatment. If you feel you need therapeutic support, we can provide guidance and referrals to qualified therapists.

Are you trauma specialists?

We are trauma-informed, meaning we understand the impact of trauma and work safely with it in mind. Whilst we are not trauma therapists, if you need specialist care, we will support you by referring you to trained professionals while continuing to hold a safe coaching space for your growth and wellness programme.

What if I need additional support outside of sessions?

You will have between-session support via our secure portal or email, so you can check in, ask questions, or get guidance on tools and practices as you need in working business hours. If it is determined that your needs would be better addressed through a consultation, we will work with you to arrange an appropriate follow-up.

Is this in-person or virtual and who is it available to?

This is primarily all done virtually, available to anyone in the UK. Dr. Heather is completely virtual. Helen offers in person cacao ceremonies with coaching, which can be booked as add-on experiences to complement and enhance your journey. She also guides people online too. If you would like to meet in person, her home studio is based in Walthamstow London E17.

What is your refund policy?

Due to the bespoke nature of this programme, sessions are non-refundable. If any issues arise, our priority is your support and we will work together to find a solution that meets your needs.
